How to Get Your File Sharing Link

Step 1: Go to Google Drive and right click the file that you want to share, then click on "Get link"
Step 2: In the window that comes up, change the visibility to "Anyone with the link"
Step 3: Click "Copy link"
Step 4: Paste that link into the text box above and click "Submit" to create your link. Enjoy!
